# Relay — Product Thesis and Goals

## Product Thesis

Traveling sports teams currently coordinate across WhatsApp threads, spreadsheets, and verbal handoffs. This system is fragile by design. Information gets lost. Confirmations are assumed. Coordinators operate on memory and stress. Players feel like afterthoughts.

Relay replaces this fragmented coordination with a structured, role-aware, confirmation-native mobile platform built for the real operational rhythm of a traveling team.

The product earns its place not by adding features, but by making the moments that matter — a player knowing they're on the trip, a coordinator knowing everyone has what they need, a coach making decisions on real information — work without effort, every time.

## Core Value Proposition

**For the coordinator:**
One place to manage a trip, communicate with the squad, collect what you need, and know who has seen what — without chasing people across four different channels.

**For the player:**
Always know where you stand — whether you're on the trip, when to show up, what to bring, and that your availability was heard and acted on.

**For the coach:**
A clear view of who is available, who is selected, and what the squad knows — without being the person who has to send every message.

**For staff:**
Access to emergency info and relevant logistics during travel, and a structured channel for communicating player restrictions.

## Product Goals

### Must Achieve in MVP
- **G1:** Coordinator can manage a complete trip without using any external tool for a critical coordination step
- **G2:** Players receive immediate structured visibility into their travel status and selection with single-tap actions
- **G3:** Coaches have a consolidated availability view enabling faster, better-informed selection decisions
- **G4:** Emergency medical info accessible to authorized staff within 3 taps during active trips, offline-tolerant
- **G5:** Structured communication feed replaces group chat for all team-critical information

### Business / Adoption Goals
- **G6:** Full squad onboarding (100% install, 80%+ profile complete) within first trip cycle
- **G7:** Coordinator completes full trip workflow without sending a single WhatsApp message for any critical step
- **G8:** Product becomes default coordination layer for the full season, not just travel events

## Non-Goals (MVP)

These must not be built, partially built, or technically scaffolded:

- General chat or open messaging
- Document file upload/storage (checklist confirmation only)
- Performance analytics, GPS load, biometrics
- Video or tactical analysis
- Scouting or recruitment
- Financial tracking or reimbursement
- Dietary/accommodation workflow (field exists; collection not built)
- Return travel as a structured workflow (fields exist; workflow not)
- Recurring trip templates
- Calendar integration
- Web coordinator dashboard
- Multi-team administration
- Fan-facing features
- Roster and eligibility compliance workflows
- AI-assisted message composition
- Player location tracking
- Attendance logging
- Language localization

## Success Metrics

### Primary (MVP Validation)

| Metric | Target | Timeframe |
|---|---|---|
| Full squad onboarding rate | ≥ 80% within first trip | Per team |
| Trip workflow completion rate | ≥ 70% run entirely in Relay | First 60 days |
| Player acknowledgment rate | ≥ 85% per trip | Per trip |
| Availability submission rate | ≥ 75% before lock | Per event |
| Selection notification open rate | ≥ 60% | Per notification |
| Coordinator 30-day retention | ≥ 65% | Per coordinator |
| Emergency info completion rate | ≥ 90% per trip | Per trip |

### Secondary
- Post acknowledgment rate by type (Schedule Update: ≥ 80%)
- Nudge effectiveness (acknowledgment within 1hr of nudge: ≥ 50%)
- Time from trip creation to first player acknowledgment (≤ 2hrs)
- Coordinator NPS ≥ 40 by end of first season

### Counter-Metrics (Warning Signals)
- Push notification denial rate > 20%
- Pending member rate at departure > 15%
- Trip created but never published > 20%
- Player availability submission rate < 50%

## MVP Success Condition

A coordinator runs a complete away trip — squad confirmed, documents checked, itinerary acknowledged, emergency info accessible — without sending a single WhatsApp message for any critical coordination step.
